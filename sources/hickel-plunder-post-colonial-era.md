## Remaining material

The paper is self-contained. Adjacent extractions in the same intellectual
tradition that would strengthen the framework's coverage of contemporary
extraction mechanisms:

- **Hickel, *The Divide* (2017)** — popular synthesis of the same
  argument with broader historical scope (colonialism, debt crisis,
  structural adjustment, neoliberalism, alternatives). Cited by this
  paper as the source for the historical material the paper assumes.
- **Patnaik & Patnaik, *A Theory of Imperialism* (2016)** — rigorous
  economic theory of imperial extraction.
- **Cope, *The Wealth of (Some) Nations* (2019)** — wage-differential
  method used as an alternative measurement in this paper.
- **Dorninger et al. (2021), "Global patterns of ecologically unequal
  exchange"** — companion empirical paper, ecological dimensions.
- **Galeano, *Open Veins of Latin America* (1971)** — primary text in
  the dependency-theory tradition this paper updates; cited in
  Background section as foundational.

PRIORITY: Patnaik & Patnaik for theoretical depth on the imperialism
question; Galeano for historical-narrative grounding and Latin American
political-economy genealogy.
