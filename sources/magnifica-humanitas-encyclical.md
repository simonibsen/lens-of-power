`principles/leo-xiv-magnifica-humanitas.md`). The remainder is read in

- **Chapters 1–2** — doctrinal history (Rerum Novarum → Vatican II) and the

  Eucharistic/Marian programme; devotional and structural, not principle-
  grade.
