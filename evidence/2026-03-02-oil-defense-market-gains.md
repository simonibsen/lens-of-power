the Iran conflict: oil and defense companies. The same pattern has
been observed during Iraq 2003, Libya 2011, and the Russia-Ukraine
war in 2022. The data is reported as market information by the BBC

which a military operation in Iran becomes a cost-of-living burden
for populations globally. The cascade operates through multiple
institutional intermediaries (oil markets, commodity markets, central
banks, retailers), making the causal relationship between war and
consumer hardship structurally invisible unless explicitly traced.
