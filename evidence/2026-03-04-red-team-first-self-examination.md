## Adjustments implemented

All three structural adjustments were applied on the same date:

1. **Axiom 10 qualified** (`constitution.md`): Restated as conditional
   — understanding resists when epistemic capacity is intact, ideology
   is not closed, and naming cannot be institutionally captured.
   Directive expanded to ask whether understanding can be absorbed,
   captured, or neutralized.

2. **Axiom 3 extended** (`constitution.md`): Fourth mode added —
   "visible mechanism, visible contingency, degraded perception" —
   covering epistemic closure (Stanley), absence of vantage point
   (Ellul), and retroactive naturalization (Schimpfössl). Falsification
   condition updated to require intact epistemic capacity. Directive
   expanded to ask whether the capacity to perceive contingency has
   been degraded.

3. **IC-2 procedurally reinforced** (`methodology.md`): Null case
   checkpoint added to Step 1 (DECOMPOSE) — analyst must state the
   strongest non-power explanation before power-dynamics mapping
   begins. Step 5 (INVERT) now explicitly revisits the initial null
   case to assess whether the analysis strengthened, weakened, or
   left it unchanged.

Adversarial input (recommendation 4) remains an operational practice
item — no structural change needed, but the next 5 analyses should
include at least 2 where the null case is likely to win.
