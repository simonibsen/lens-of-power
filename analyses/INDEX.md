# Analysis Index

> Role: Registry of all completed analyses. Used to detect selection bias
> (IC-3), identify clustering, and track null case outcomes. Review this
> index during red team reviews.
>
> **Column definitions**:
> - **Date**: When the analysis was conducted
> - **Material**: What was analyzed (article, policy, event, etc.)
> - **Domain**: Subject area (foreign policy, platform governance, etc.)
> - **Primary layers**: Which taxonomy layers (from `taxonomy.md`) were
>   most active in the material — e.g., Physical & Coercive, Thought &
>   Narrative, Economic, Legal & Regulatory, Institutional, Surveillance
>   & Information
> - **Null case outcome**: Whether the non-power explanation (IC-2) was
>   rejected, plausible, or accepted. The null case asks: "Can this be
>   explained without power dynamics — by incompetence, accident, inertia,
>   or good faith?" If the null case column never reads "plausible" or
>   "accepted," the framework may be functioning as a confirmation machine
>   (see IC-3 in `constitution.md`).
